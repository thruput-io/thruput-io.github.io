Brand Guidelines

Core principles for maintaining brand consistency.

  • ✓

    Clarity First

    Always prioritize clear communication over decorative elements. Every design choice should serve a functional purpose. The "no fuss" philosophy extends to all brand touchpoints.

  • 🌱

    Sustainable Visual Language

    Use the green palette purposefully to reinforce our environmental commitment. Avoid greenwashing—the colors should reflect genuine sustainability, not just trend.

  • ⚡

    Performance-Oriented

    Digital assets should be optimized and efficient. Use web-safe formats, compress images properly, and avoid unnecessary animations or heavy resources.

  • 📐

    Consistent Spacing

    Maintain generous white space and consistent padding/margins. Use an 8px grid system for alignment. Let content breathe—overcrowding undermines our efficiency message.

  • 🎯

    Purposeful Accents

    Amber is reserved for calls-to-action and important highlights. Don't overuse it—strategic placement makes it more effective. Think of it as a spotlight, not ambient lighting.

  • ✏️

    Typography Hierarchy

    Use Bricolage Grotesque for headlines and emphasis (weight 600-800). DM Sans for body copy (weight 400-600). Avoid mixing more than 2 weights in a single composition.

Don'ts

  • ✗ Don't stretch, skew, or distort the wordmark
  • ✗ Don't use low-contrast color combinations that hurt readability
  • ✗ Don't add drop shadows, gradients, or effects to the logo
  • ✗ Don't place the logo on busy backgrounds or complex imagery
  • ✗ Don't use colors outside the defined palette
  • ✗ Don't create decorative elements that don't serve a clear purpose
